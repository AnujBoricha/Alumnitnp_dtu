Why Donate

80+ years of Tradition of excellence in Engineering & Technology Education, Research and Innovations" Delhi Technological University is catering the needs of Indian industries for trained technical manpower with practical experience and sound theoretical knowledge.

To carry out this tradition of excellence we rely on the generosity of our alumni to pool our resources together to create new foot prints in the form of new building blocks and academic and research facilities in their alma-mater. In a way helping the student community to emulate some of the best practices with which the illustrious alumni of our great institution have earned high eminence both nationally as well as globally.

We welcome donations of any size, any amount and they will be used to fulfill the vision and mission of DTU:

  1. To be a world class centre for education, research and innovations in S&T arena, focus on cutting edge technologies for education delivery and foster an environment of seamlessness between science and technology.
  2. Promote the engineering spirit of product development through effective integration of design engineering and rapid prototyping.
  3. Emerge as a centre for attracting the best minds both nationally and internationally, from academia and industry.
  4. Produce well-rounded, socially responsible, up to date, scientifically tempered, design oriented engineer and scientists capable of lifelong learning.
  5. To be able to address the impact of globalization on S&T education.
  6. Meet the need for engineers and scientists at all levels of tertiary education, UG, PG, and Doctoral levels.
